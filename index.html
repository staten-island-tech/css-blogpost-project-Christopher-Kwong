Famous Places to Visit in NYC

Times Square

As one of the most iconic tourist destinations in NYC, Times Square is a must see. With its busy streets and bright lights, it's a sight to behold. The shops and businesses there offer a variety of entertainment ranging from food to things like skating.

Statue of Liberty

Yet another one of the most iconic tourist destinations in NYC, the Statue of Liberty is located at Liberty Island. There, one can enter the statue itself if they have a ticket. In addition, the original torch of the statue can be found in the museum right next door.

Central Park

As the largest park in Manhattan, it contains a great deal of greenery that can't be found anywhere else in the city. It's a place to relax and admire the gardens and architecture. There's also a castle and even an ice skating rink found within the park!
